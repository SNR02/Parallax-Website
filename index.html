Dubai - United Arab Emirates

About




Dubai, the jewel of the United Arab Emirates, has become synonymous with luxury, innovation, and unparalleled experiences. This spectacular metropolis is the perfect destination for travelers seeking a unique and unforgettable journey. With its captivating fusion of ancient traditions and ultramodern architecture, Dubai offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the future while preserving the essence of its rich cultural heritage.

The city's skyline is a testament to human ingenuity, featuring awe-inspiring structures such as the iconic Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, and the Burj Al Arab, a sail-shaped hotel that has come to symbolize Dubai's opulence. Visitors can ascend to the observation deck on the 148th floor of the Burj Khalifa for a breathtaking panoramic view of the city and beyond.

Dubai is also a haven for shoppers, boasting an array of extravagant malls, such as the Dubai Mall, which is one of the largest shopping centers in the world, and the Mall of the Emirates, home to an indoor ski resort. Traditional souks offer an authentic taste of the region's culture, where travelers can immerse themselves in the lively atmosphere and haggle for goods like gold, spices, and textiles.

For those seeking adventure, the Arabian Desert awaits just outside the city, providing a striking contrast to the urban landscape. Travelers can embark on thrilling excursions such as dune bashing, camel riding, and sandboarding, or experience the tranquility of a desert safari beneath the stars. Dubai's pristine beaches, like Jumeirah Beach and Kite Beach, offer a refreshing respite from the desert heat and an opportunity to partake in water sports or simply soak up the sun.

Culinary enthusiasts will find themselves spoilt for choice in Dubai, which is home to an impressive array of dining options. From world-class restaurants helmed by celebrity chefs to traditional Emirati cuisine, there is a gastronomic experience to suit every palate. The city's diverse population is reflected in its culinary offerings, which encompass flavors from around the globe.

Dubai is also renowned for its extravagant events and festivals, such as the Dubai Shopping Festival, Dubai International Film Festival, and Dubai World Cup, which attract visitors from all corners of the world. The city's vibrant nightlife scene is another major draw, featuring luxurious clubs, rooftop bars, and exclusive lounges where visitors can dance the night away or simply enjoy a cocktail with a view.

For those interested in the arts, Dubai is a flourishing cultural hub. The Alserkal Avenue district, for example, is home to numerous art galleries and creative spaces, showcasing works by both local and international artists. The Dubai Opera, an architectural masterpiece, hosts a variety of performances, from ballet and opera to musicals and concerts.

Burj Al Arab

Burj Al Arab is one of the premium hotels in Dubai, located on an artificial island off Jumeirah Road. Designed in the shape of a dhow sail, this iconic landmark houses an array of shiny chauffeur-driven limousines and a private helipad.

University of Sharjah

The University of Sharjah is an Emirati private national university located in University City, Sharjah, United Arab Emirates. It was established in October 1997 by its founder the ruler of Sharjah Sheikh Dr. Sultan bin Muhammad Al-Qasimi.

Dubai Fountain is the world's largest choreographed water fountain. This dancing fountain surges as high as 150 meters in complete synchronisation to classical, Arabic and other popular world musical numbers.
